Figure N. The coordination pipeline on the coordination space. Sense builds awareness in personal space---both the current state of relationships and the consequences of potential changes. Gate determines whether the change stays personal (within boundary, writer proceeds individually) or escalates to the team (crosses boundary). Negotiate structures team deliberation and resolution; the updated BNA reshapes writing, triggering new sensing. The pipeline cycles continuously as writing progresses; the Gate is the configurable boundary between individual autonomy and team coordination.
