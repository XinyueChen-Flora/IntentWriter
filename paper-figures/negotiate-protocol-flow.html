Figure N. A negotiate protocol: declaration (left) and stage-by-stage runtime (right). Propose: the proposer runs frame-proposal to structure the change with context and reasoning. Deliberate: each voter sees the framed proposal and a preview of how their own writing would change (rendered by preview-writing-impact); voters approve, reject, or counter-propose. Resolve: when the team-configured resolution rule is met (here, majority), the BNA updates and the pipeline cycles back to Sense. Teams configure who votes, what resolution rule to use, and the deadline.
